Web accessibility

Web accessibility means that websites, tools, and technologies are designed and developed so that people with disabilities can use them.

Why it is important?

  • 15% of people have a disability that could affect how they browse the web
  • The concept of accessibility doesn’t just apply to people with disabilities – all users will have different needs at different times and in different circumstances.

Some statistics...

  • 4.5% of the global population experience color blindness, 4% suffer from low vision, and 0,6% are completely blind.
  • over 5% of the world’s population has a disabling hearing loss
  • it’s estimated that by 2050 over 900 million people (1 in 10 people) will have a hearing impairment
